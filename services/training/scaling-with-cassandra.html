Why Attend?

Three drivers are moving software development into Big Data arena: Internet scale, Analytics and IoT. Traditional RDBMS solutions are inefficient for those challenges and companies like Netflix or Ebay have chosen Cassandra as a proved solution that works.

Low latency and high throughput are key factors for modern apps. Cassandra favours those over storage as commodity servers deployed on elastic cloud providers makes that trade-off really convenient.

Customers expect zero downtime and high availability. Cassandra distributed architecture with tunable replication and no single point of failure is a perfect fit for achieving that operational excelence.

Mixing concerns like writes and reads leads to brittle applications and eventually to ORM hell. Cassandra enforces a flexible and performant data modeling style that suits well with architectures like CQRS or Event Sourcing.

This course will give you the know-how through hands-on sessions on data modeling. We'll inspect locally deployed Cassandra clusters to confirm theoretical insights presented in the course.
